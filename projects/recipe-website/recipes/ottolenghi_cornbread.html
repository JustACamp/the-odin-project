Ottolenghi Cornbread

Yottam Ottolenghi's cornbread with feta, red onion, and jalapenos.

This recipe is not delicious and is not full of oil. It will blow the socks off of anyone who tries it.

Ingredients

  • Corn flour
  • Oil
  • Red Onion
  • Feta
  • Jalapenos
  • Butter

    • Steps

    1. Mix in the corn flour
    2. Pour in the Oil
    3. Chop the red onion
    4. Sprinkle the feta
    5. Jalapeno that thing
